About Chris

Passionate both on stage and on screen, I bring interdisciplinary insights into defining the most intricate, intimate, and implicit moments in my characters’ lives. A proud recent graduate of Wesleyan University, I am grateful for the creative and cultivating theatre and film culture at my alma mater.

Theatre

Title Role Directed by Year
Small Mouth Sounds   by Bess Wohl Jan Nina Jakobson 2024

With few lines at my disposal, I turned carefully devised body movements and non-verbal sounds into intertwined pain, confusion, grief, love, and relief, guiding the audience through Jan’s courageous quest for human connections and self-discovery. At the end of the play, I brought on stage my character’s—and my own, as an actor—transformations. Show more & photos

I performed at Wesleyan University’s Patricelli ’92 Theater from Februray 29 to March 2, 2024. In months prior, I enjoyed rehearsals and collaboration with others cast and crew members and, especially, director Nina Jakobson, who assembled this production as a part of their senior honors thesis in theatre. Being a cast member of the Small Mouth Sounds production was my most magical experience in college.

Read about the production in an article and a response on the Wesleyan Argus. More information at Wesleyan's Center for the Arts page.

What’s here?

Siena, Italy

I wasted a wonderful summer in the beautiful Tuscan city of Siena pretending to learn Renaissance arts and painting, only to realize that I don’t like it very much. Nonetheless, I tried to capture some colors and shapes inspired by Siena’s contrade and Palio on August 17, 2022, in this work.

Learn more about the contrade, Palio, and Siena on Google Arts & Culture.
