About Chris

Passionate both on stage and on screen, I bring interdisciplinary insights into defining the most intricate, intimate, and implicit moments in my characters’ lives. A proud recent graduate of Wesleyan University, I am grateful for the creative and cultivating theatre and film culture at my alma mater.

Theatre

Title Role Directed by Year
Small Mouth Sounds Jan Nina Jakobson 2024

In this breviloquent yet powerful piece by Bess Wohl, Jan is the first charater to enter and the last to leave. With few words at my disposal, I turned my conscientiously devised body movements and non-verbal sounds into layered voices, guiding the audience through my character’s couragous quest for human connections and self discovery. Portraying Jan’s intertwined confusion, pain, relief, grief, and deeply touching love, I brought on stage his – and my own, as an actor – transformations.

Read about the production on Wesleyan's Center for the Arts page, which is also covered by the Wesleyan Argus in an article and a response.
